FINANCE TEAM

The FINANCE TEAM is responsible for all things financial within the ORS, including negotiating benefits for members, drawing up yearly and termly budgets, and leading financial and business strategy to help the ORS grow and achieve our goals.

The EVENTS TEAM organises all our large events, dealing with invites, events logistics and leading our other teams to prepare design and marketing material. They arrange a diverse selection of our events, from speaker events & workshops to club nights and gigs.
